Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about the Cyber UXcellence Awards.

General Information

Who is eligible to submit a nomination?

+

Product managers, chief product officers, and teams responsible for developing cybersecurity products are eligible to submit nominations. If your product excels in user experience and interface design, you can nominate it for the Cyber UXcellence Awards. The awards are designed to recognize security product managers and teams who build exceptional security product user experiences.

Who is organizing the Cyber UXcellence Awards?

+

The Cyber UXcellence Awards are organized by Mindgrub, a leader in technology innovation, Product UX/UI, and digital transformation. Mindgrub is dedicated to promoting exceptional user experiences in cybersecurity and highlighting the products that are setting the standard for innovation and usability in the industry.

Is there an associated cost with submitting?

+

No, there are no costs associated with submitting a nomination for the Cyber UXcellence Awards. Judging is conducted independently by a panel of industry experts, ensuring fairness and integrity in the selection process.

Nomination Process

What materials are required for nomination?

+

To submit a nomination, you will need to provide detailed information about your product's UX and UI features, its impact on users, and any supporting materials such as user testimonials, case studies, or product demos or video clips that highlight the product strengths.

What is the deadline for submissions?

+

The deadline for submitting nominations is June 27, 2025. All submissions must be completed by this date.

How long does it take to complete the nomination process?

+

The nomination process is fast, intuitive, and flexible. Our user-friendly online platform allows you to start, save, and refine your submissions with ease. You can also duplicate entries across different categories, making it quick and simple to nominate your product in one or multiple award categories.

Can we submit the same product for multiple categories?

+

Yes, you can submit your product for multiple categories if it meets the criteria for each one.

Judging & Selection

How are winners selected?

+

Submissions are evaluated by a distinguished panel of judges representing top industry CISOs, UX experts, and cybersecurity thought leaders.

Each submission is assessed based on predefined published rubric criteria, including UX/UI design, usability, adoption rates, impact on security outcomes, and overall user experience. Judges evaluate entries according to the specific category descriptions.

When will I know if I won or am a finalist?

+

Winners will be notified via email during the judging period and will be announced at the awards ceremony during Black Hat 2025 in Las Vegas. You do not need to attend the Black Hat event to win. Winners will be recognized and celebrated during the awards ceremony at Black Hat 2025.
